Splendid storytelling and dialog. Beautiful photography in this over all feel good drama/thriller. This movie describes the mentality, and the way of life in a small town in the middle of Sweden in a superb way. All the way to the thrilling end of the story.<br /><br />Great acting from booth Jonas Karlsson and Helena af Sandeberg.<br /><br />This is a must see movie for everybody who lived in the 50-60th and a should see movie for all the rest. It's not every day you watch a drama-thriller with this type of please tell me more I want more feeling.<br /><br />To sum it up: I must say I love it. And I advise you to see it.
