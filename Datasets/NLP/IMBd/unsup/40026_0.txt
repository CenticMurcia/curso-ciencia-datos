A young virginal bride, an old haunted castle, and a twisted sexual curse! Sounds good? Don't bet on it. There are a few scares in this ponderous adaptation of the novel FENGRIFFEN, but they are very few and far between. By the time the family secret was revealed I had lost all interest.<br /><br />One more thing.What does the title 'And Now the Screaming Starts' have to do with the movie?<br /><br />You've got me.
