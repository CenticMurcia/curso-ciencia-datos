Where else can you see a film with Ron Jeremy, Lou Diamond Phillips and Scott Thompson?? This is a hilarious send up of the porn world. It's a comedy, so there's no nudity, but that's the whole point. Bill and Earl have turned the porn world into a family friendly environment..except for the last porn scene which gets...a little messy. The writing is strong, the acting is so sincere, and the women are gorgeous. I heard this short is being turned into an animated series where Bill and Earl are super heroes. Looking forward to seeing it. Anyone who wants to check this short out for themselves can see it at the atom films site. Enjoy!
