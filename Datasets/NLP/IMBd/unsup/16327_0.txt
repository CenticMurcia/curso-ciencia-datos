My wife liked it, the two lesbians sitting behind us loved it, but I wouldn't say it was the best film I have ever seen.<br /><br />PLOT I thought this was pretty simplistic. I found this pretty surprising as the film East is East had them but this did not.<br /><br />ACTING Not bad. I cannot remember what the actors names were, but they were OK, not outstanding.<br /><br />CINEMATOGRAPHY Pretty good overall. Use of lighting was good.
