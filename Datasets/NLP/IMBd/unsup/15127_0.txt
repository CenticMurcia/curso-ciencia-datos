Note: Possible Spoiler.<br /><br />Terrific film with absolutely standout performances from both Bob Hoskins and Helen Mirren, along with a fine ensemble of lesser-known castmembers. The much-talked-about climax in this movie is surely one of the most enthralling and riveting in the history of cinema -- and this is no exaggeration! Bob Hoskings virtuoso performance in the final scene transcends mere acting or descriptive superlatives. He is just stunning. In a brief few seconds, Hoskings exudes surprise, fear, realization and acceptance as to....... The accompanying pulsating musical score from composer Francis Monkman gives further power and emotional resonance to this scene. Your heart simply races along with Hoskins' "ride".
