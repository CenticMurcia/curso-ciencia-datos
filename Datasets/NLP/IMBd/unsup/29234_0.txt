The John Wayne Marathon continues into the 14th hour with a whole boatload of interesting characters in a story about the gold rush in Alaska.<br /><br />Wayne gets third billing in this movie to Marlene Dietrich and Randolph Scott. Dietrich plays a jealous girlfriend and Scott is the new dude in town that is muscling in.<br /><br />Margaret Lindsay plays the new girl in town after Wayne's heart. Harry Carey is Wayne's partner.<br /><br />This is at least the 4th version of this story and it won't be the last. A tale of gold mining, claim jumping, saloon, muddy streets, brawls and fancy women will always be in style.<br /><br />Hang on to your hat and enjoy another Wayne classic.
