There are so many many stories to tell... If These Walls Could Talk 2 did an incredible job of telling the stories that they chose. The characters were true to life, and empathetic. It takes you through a full range of emotions in less than two hours. People need to know that lesbians are people too. We have joys, and sorrows just like you. And some of the heartache that we have had to endure is imposed by society and altogether unnecessary.
